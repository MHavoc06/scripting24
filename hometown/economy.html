ECONOMY

The UNESCO World Heritage City of Bruges welcomes as many as 8.3 million visitors annually. Combined with a world port, a healthcare sector with 800 years of history and complemented by great expertise in ICT and mechatronics, it is a popular home for a great many sectors.

These economic growth sectors are the holy grail of the Bruges economy. They are the perfect breeding ground for attracting exciting national and international residential conferences and other tourists’ attractions’ activities.
