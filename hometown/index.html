TO BELGIUM

Bruges is the capital and largest city of the province of West Flanders in the Flemish Region of Belgium, in the northwest of the country. It is the sixth most populated city in the country.

Along with the other canal-based northern cities, such as Amsterdam and Saint Peterburg, it is sometimes referred to as the “Venice of the North”.

Bruges was a location of coastal settlement during the prehistoric period, This Bronze Age and Iron Age settlement are unrelated to medieval city development.

Bruges is home to many museums. Its art museum includes the Arents house, as well as the Groeninge museum, which has an extensive collection of medieval and early modern art.

Bruges is a major tourist destination within Belgium and well known as the seats of the College of Europe, a university institute of European studies.
